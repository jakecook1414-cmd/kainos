What is Kainos Deep?

A nightly sleep and recovery powder with nine clinically dosed ingredients — transparent labeling, no proprietary blends, thirty servings per tub.

Who is it for?

Adults who train hard, work long days, and want to wake up restored — not wired, not groggy. If you’re pregnant, nursing, or on medication, ask your doctor first.

We’re in pre-launch for 2026. Waitlist members hear first when orders open and receive founding codes before standard retail.
