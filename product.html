Ingredient rationale

Built with intention.
Dosed for science, not marketing.

L-Glycine — 3,000mg

Lowers core body temperature to accelerate deep sleep onset. The primary reason Kainos Deep is a powder — this dose cannot fit in a capsule. Most competitors skip it entirely.

L-Glutamine — 2,000mg

Supports overnight gut lining repair and muscle recovery. Pairs with glycine for a complete overnight rebuild stack that no capsule can carry at these doses.

Ashwagandha KSM-66® — 600mg

Cortisol is the #1 reason people can't sleep. KSM-66 is the most clinically studied form of ashwagandha in the world. Full 600mg — never compromised.

VitaCherry® Tart cherry — 500mg

15x more concentrated than generic tart cherry. Reduces muscle soreness and supports the body's natural melatonin pathway. Where sleep and recovery meet in one ingredient.

L-Theanine — 200mg

Promotes alpha brain wave activity — the calm state you feel after meditation. Quiets the racing mind without grogginess or dependency.

Apigenin — 50mg

Chamomile-derived. Modulates GABA-A receptors to initiate relaxation in the first 30 minutes — before the deeper ingredients fully activate.

Magnesium bisglycinate — 250mg elemental

Supports GABA activity, muscle relaxation, and melatonin regulation. Bisglycinate absorbs far better than oxide — and is gentle on the stomach for nightly use.

Zinc bisglycinate — 11mg

Supports melatonin synthesis and overnight muscle and immune function. Bisglycinate form — actually absorbed, not just listed on the label.

Melatonin — 0.5mg

We use 0.5mg. Not 5mg. Not 10mg. Low-dose melatonin is as effective for sleep onset without suppressing natural production. Every competitor floods the system. Kainos guides it.

How to use

Simple. Consistent. Every night.

Tap a row for timing, consistency, and everyday guidance.

01 · Measure one scoop

One level scoop (~6.6g) per serving. 30 servings per tub.

02 · Mix with cold water

Add to 8–10oz of cold water. Shake well for 20–30 seconds until fully dissolved.

03 · Drink before bed

Take 30–60 minutes before bedtime. Use nightly for best results.

Best time to take

30–60 minutes before your intended sleep time. Apigenin and L-Theanine begin working within the first 30 minutes.

Consistency matters

Ashwagandha KSM-66 and magnesium build over time. Most people notice the biggest difference after 7–14 days of nightly use.

Safe for daily use

All 9 ingredients are well-tolerated for nightly use. Melatonin is dosed at 0.5mg — low enough not to suppress your body's natural production.

Who it's for

Adults who train, work hard, and want to wake up genuinely restored. If you're pregnant, nursing, or on medication — consult your doctor first.
