Built by an Athlete,
For Athletes

"I didn't sleep well for two years of college football. I knew there had to be a better answer than melatonin gummies."

Jake Cook played collegiate football and watched recovery be treated as an afterthought. As a finance major at TCU, he brought the same discipline he applied to the field into building a brand designed to close the gap between effort and recovery. Kainos Labs is the product he wished existed — and a company built to last.
